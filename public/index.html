I built this so my friends and I can see what each other is listening to. Every time you like a song on Spotify, it shows up here for the group to see, play, and comment on.

To get started: Click "+ Connect Spotify" and log in with your Spotify account. That's it. Your liked songs will start appearing in the feed.
